What are crinkle cookies?

Crinkle cookies are delicious desserts with a moist, brownie-like texture in the inside and have a distinctive powdered sugar coating. These delicious treats are popular in the Philippines.

Chocolate is the most common and popular crinkle cookie flavour, although crinkle cookies are now available in various exciting flavours.

Currently, CrinkleSprinkle is able to offer two flavours, chocolate and ube. But stay tuned as we test more exotic flavours.
